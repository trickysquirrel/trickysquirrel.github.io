Why Salience Maps?

Salience maps identify the parts of an image that are most likely to grab a viewer’s attention. Whether you’re preparing images for design projects, presentations, or just want to see where the focus naturally falls, this app helps you make data-driven decisions.

Harness the potential of machine learning and make your images stand out with a click. Download now and start creating attention-focused images in seconds!
