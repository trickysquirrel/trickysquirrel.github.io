Description

By changing where we grab a coffee or food once a month we can make real IMPACT in many peoples lives. All cafes in this app support communities in need. Help them help others.

Features:

  • List of cafes and restaurants in Melbourne
  • Set a reminder to keep up the habit
  • Map
  • Directions
