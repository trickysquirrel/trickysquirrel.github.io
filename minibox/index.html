Description

We want to provide a safe environment for young children to watch YouTube channels without worrying about whether they will stumble across something unsuitable for their age. All YouTube channels have been selected and vetted by parents with children under 5.

They can enjoy all their favourite shows such as Peppa Pig, Masha and The Bear, Sesame Street and much more!

This application is designed for under 5's to easily navigate, discover and play videos, and as it the safest environment we can make, you can feel comfortable to let them use the app on their own.

We have worked hard to select the best kids channels, but it is possible that the channel owners present something that you may think is not suitable. If this is the case please let us know as it will make a better experience for everyone.
