Description

Protect Your Privacy

Identifying and remove personal and private information from text, PDF documents, and images on your Mac. Designed with privacy in mind, this app ensures your sensitive data remains confidential, preventing misuse by free AI tools that could exploit your information.

Key Features:
- Comprehensive Detection: Automatically scan documents for addresses, phone numbers, links, and more.
- Customisable Regex: Define your own regular expressions to detect specific patterns unique to your needs.
- Ignore List: Easily exclude known dummy data from scans, ensuring accuracy and efficiency.
- Secure Replacement: Export documents with automatic replacements for sensitive text, keeping your information safe.
- Privacy First: No data is transfered outside of this app, guaranteeing your personal information remains secure.

Why Choose this app?
- Protect Your Privacy: Safeguard your personal information before uploading files to any online tools.
- User-Friendly Interface: Easily manage your documents and sensitive information with an intuitive interface.
- Peace of Mind: Trust that your data stays on your device, with no external usage or sharing.

Perfect tool for anyone looking to maintain their privacy in an increasingly digital world. Download now and take control of your personal information today!
