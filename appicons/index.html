Description

Introducing AppIcons, a Mac app that allows you to create stunning app icons with just a simple description using the power of AI. With just a few clicks, you can describe your app icon using words like “cute cat”, “happy hamster” and let our AI system generate a beautiful and unique icon that perfectly captures the essence of your brand. Whether you're a seasoned app developer or just starting out you can start creating icons that make your app stand out from the crowd.

With a lot of features:

  • Icons delivered in less than 1 minute
  • Size 1024 x 1024
  • Variety of +20 icon styles
  • Save images at various sizes specific to iOS/Android apps
  • Select up to 10 images to be generated at the same time
  • Full ownership of the generated icons
